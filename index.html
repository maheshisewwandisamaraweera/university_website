Course We Offer

The University offers a diverse range of courses to equip students for success.

Computer Science

The Computer Science program at the University provides a comprehensive education in software development, algorithms, and data structures. Students gain hands-on experience through projects and internships, preparing them for careers in technology and innovation.

Business Administration

The Business Administration course equips students with essential skills in management, finance, and marketing. Emphasizing real-world applications and leadership, the program prepares graduates for successful careers in diverse business environments.

Environmental Science

The Environmental Science program offers an in-depth understanding of ecological systems, sustainability, and environmental policy. Students engage in fieldwork and research, addressing critical environmental challenges and promoting sustainable practices.
